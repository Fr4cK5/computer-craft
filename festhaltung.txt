
/// Step 1: Sulfur Dust
/give -> brine
10mb brine -> 1mb sodium + **1mb chlorine**
1mb chlorine + 1mb hydrogen -> 1mb hydrogen chloride
200mb hydrogen chloride + 1 gun powder -> **1 sulfur dust**

/// Step 2: Sulfur Dioxide

1 sulfur dust -> 100mb sulfur dioxide

/// Step 3: Sulfur Trioxide

2mb water -> 2mb hydrogen + **1mb oxygen**
1mb oxygen + 2mb sulfur dioxide -> 2mb sulfur trioxide

/// Step 4: Sulfuric Acid

1mb water -> 1mb water vapor
1mb water vapor + 1mb sulfur trioxide -> 1mb sulfuric acid 

/// Step 5: Slurry

3 erz + 100mb sulfuric acid -> 2000mb slurry

/// Step 6: Clean Slurry

1mb slurry + 5mb water -> 1mb clean slurry

/// Step 7: Ingot 

200mb clean slurry -> 1 crystal

1 crystal -> 1 shard
1 shard -> 1 clump
1 clump -> 1 dirty dust
1 dirty dust -> 1 clean dust

1 clean dust -> 1 ingot
